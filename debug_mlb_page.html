Frequently Asked Questions

What MLB team has the most regular season wins all time?

The record is 116 wins, shared by the 1906 Cubs and the 2001 Mariners. However, the Cubs did it in just 155 games, while the Mariners played 162.

What is the only current MLB team to never play in a World Series?

The Seattle Mariners are the only current MLB team to never play in a World Series.

Who won 116 games in MLB?

Two teams achieved this feat. The 1906 Cubs and the 2001 Mariners.

What is the most MLB wins in a single season?

The record is 116 wins, shared by the 1906 Cubs and the 2001 Mariners.

What is the best 162 game record in MLB history?

It is 116-46, a record posted by the Mariners in 2001. They are two wins ahead of the 1998 Yankees, who finished 114-48.

What is the best season record in MLB history?

The 1906 Cubs went 116-36, for a .763 W-L%, the best record in an MLB season.
